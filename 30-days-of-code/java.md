## Day of 23 - BST Level-Order Traversal
### Notes
level order like:
```
			5
	4				8
1		3		6		9
```
related with tree height level, follow the steps:
>1. use queue reslove it.
>2. queue offer root.
>3. while queue not empty.
>4. poll from queue and print it.
>5. travel from left to right.

answer is:
`5 4 8 1 3 6 9`
